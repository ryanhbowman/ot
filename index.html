Founded in 2016 with BBC Studios as a strategic investor, Original Talent is a combined agency, production and management group that aims to bring together the finest writers, actors, directors, presenters, performers, below the line talent and literary material on one platform.

Group Companies

Original Talent’s ethos is to encourage autonomy within its family. We believe in retaining the unique qualities and values of each company in the group, but also allowing clients to benefit from the wider opportunity and exposure that comes from scale and shared resources, information and talent.
